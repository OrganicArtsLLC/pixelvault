SHT-001 · SWARM RESCUE

Mechanic: Twin-stick — move and shoot independently in 8 directions

Ancestry: Robotron: 2084 (1982, Williams), Smash TV (1990), Geometry Wars (2003)

Controls:

  • WASD — Move
  • Arrow keys — Shoot direction (8-way)
  • R — Restart
  • ? — Toggle this panel

Rules: Survive waves of enemies. Rescue humans (green) for bonus points before hulks (purple) crush them. Grunts chase you. Brains reprogram humans into enemies. Spheroids spawn enforcers. Clear all enemies to advance.

AI Insight: Twin-stick decoupled movement from attack for the first time. Before Robotron, you moved toward danger to fight it. After, you could flee and fight simultaneously. This split the player's attention into two parallel control streams — cognitive load as game design.
