SHT-005 · ROYALE

SHT — Shooter Prototype #005 ★★★★☆

How to Play

You are the green triangle. Survive against 20 AI opponents in a shrinking arena. Loot weapons and supplies. Last one standing wins.

  • WASD — Move
  • Mouse — Aim
  • Click — Shoot
  • E — Pick up nearby item
  • R — Restart
  • ? — This info panel

Weapons

  • Pistol — Starting weapon, moderate damage, unlimited ammo
  • Shotgun — Close range, high damage, spread shot
  • Rifle — Fast fire rate, medium damage
  • Sniper — Long range, high damage, slow fire

Zone

The blue safe zone shrinks in stages. Outside the zone, you take increasing storm damage. Stay inside to survive. A white circle on the minimap shows the next zone.

History

Battle royale games exploded in 2017 with PUBG and Fortnite, but the concept traces to H1Z1 (2015) and the ARMA mod that inspired it. The genre took the last-man-standing concept from films like Battle Royale (2000) and turned it into a new competitive format.

AI Archaeology

Transformer Era (ERA 6)

The shrinking zone is a masterclass in emergent gameplay design. A single spatial constraint — reducing available territory over time — solves camping, guarantees dramatic convergence, and creates natural pacing without scripted events. AI opponents here use simple heuristics (seek loot, avoid storm, engage nearby enemies) yet produce believable behavior through environmental pressure.

Classification

top-down shooter battle royale survival shrinking zone loot

Prototype Notes

Distills the battle royale formula to its essential elements: shrinking zone, scattered loot, and last-standing win condition. The 2000x2000 arena with 21 players captures the genre's tension in miniature. AI opponents fight each other, creating organic drama.
