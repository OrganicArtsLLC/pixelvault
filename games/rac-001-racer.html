RAC-001 · RACER

Mechanic: Pseudo-3D perspective road — steer, accelerate, avoid

Ancestry: Night Driver (1976), Pole Position (1982), Out Run (1986)

Controls:

  • Left / Right — Steer
  • Up — Accelerate
  • Down — Brake
  • R — Restart
  • ? — Toggle this panel

Goal: Race as far as possible before time runs out. Pass other cars to earn bonus time. Hitting roadside objects or other cars slows you down dramatically.

AI Insight: Pseudo-3D is one of gaming's great optical illusions. No 3D engine needed — just scaled horizontal lines creating the sensation of depth. The road is a mathematical curve rendered as stacked trapezoids.
