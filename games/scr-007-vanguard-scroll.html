SCR-007 · VANGUARD SCROLL

SCR — Scrolling Shooter Prototype #007 ★★★★☆

How to Play

Pilot your ship through five hostile zones. Scroll direction changes each zone. Shoot in all four directions to survive. Collect energy pickups to stay alive and gain brief invincibility.

  • Arrow keys — Move ship (auto-fires in movement direction)
  • Z — Fire left
  • X — Fire right
  • C — Fire up
  • V — Fire down
  • Space — Activate energy shield (drains energy fast)
  • R — Restart
  • ? — Toggle this info panel

Tips

  • Energy: Depletes over time. Collect yellow "E" pickups to refill and gain brief invincibility.
  • Invincibility: When flashing rainbow, you are invulnerable and destroy enemies on contact.
  • Zones change direction: The scroll direction shifts each zone — stay alert for the transition.
  • 4-way fire: Use all four directions — enemies come from everywhere.
  • Boss: The Gond awaits in the City of Mystery. It takes many hits.

History & Lineage

Ancestry: Scramble (1981, Konami) → Vanguard (1981, TOSE/SNK) → Bosconian (1981, Namco)

Vanguard (1981) by TOSE, published by SNK, was one of the earliest continuous-scrolling shooters and one of the first to allow firing in four directions simultaneously. The game was notable for changing its scroll direction between zones — horizontal, vertical, and diagonal — keeping the player perpetually off-balance.

It borrowed Scramble's fuel mechanic but replaced it with an energy system that doubled as an invincibility power-up. Collecting energy pickups triggered a brief window of invulnerability accompanied by a distinctive rainbow flash and the "Vultan's Theme" from Flash Gordon — one of the first uses of licensed music in a video game.

The multi-directional scrolling and 4-way fire anticipated the design space that later games like Bosconian, Time Pilot, and eventually twin-stick shooters would explore.

Core Mechanic

Multi-directional scroll with 4-way fire and energy management. Scroll direction changes per zone. Four simultaneous fire directions. Energy as both resource and invincibility trigger. Boss encounter at finale.
