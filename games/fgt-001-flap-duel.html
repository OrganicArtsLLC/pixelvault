FGT-001 · JOUST

Mechanic: Flap-to-fly — height advantage wins lance combat

Ancestry: Joust (1982, Williams), Balloon Fight (1984, Nintendo)

Controls:

  • Space / Up — Flap (gain height)
  • Left / Right — Move horizontally
  • R — Restart
  • ? — Toggle this panel

Rules: Defeat enemies by being HIGHER than them when you collide. If they're higher, you lose a life. Defeated enemies drop eggs — collect them before they hatch into new enemies. Clear all enemies to advance waves.

AI Insight: Height-wins reduces combat to a single axis of advantage. The flap mechanic creates emergent difficulty — mastering momentum IS the game. No health bars needed when physics determines victory.
